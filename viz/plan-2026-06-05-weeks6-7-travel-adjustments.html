—— Block 01 · week-by-week ——

The rebuild is done.
Now comes the first quality.

Seven weeks since Tuscany, three since Kungsholmen: the aerobic engine is back, both long runs (24 km May 22, 29 km May 29) landed as clean Z2 negative splits. The one gap the May 30 review named — no on-purpose quality since the race — is now closed: Tuesday Jun 2 delivered a real 4×4 VO₂, ran deliberate and felt good, and Friday Jun 5's 31.6 km long backed it up with drift 0.36. Block 1's long-run progression (24 → 29 → 31.6 km) closes exactly where the rebuild aimed.

Standing rule (Tuesday): the work group sets the format each week — 4×4 min, 5×6 min, or easy night, Kundo's call. Patrik tunes the paces to the cycle: build week → reps at prescribed effort; recovery week → same reps jogged at recovery pace; race/post-race week → easy with the group or skip. The plan stops trying to replace Tuesday with a different prescription. Group runs are always 8–10 km, never more.

Standing rule (Fri / weekend zones): Friday long run is explicit Z2 — HR 140–150. The daughter weekend ~21 km is explicit Z1 — HR < 140 — daughter's 6:00–6:30 /km lands it in Z1 naturally; it doesn't count as the week's long-run stimulus.

Adopted May 10: 2× / week strength. Heavy stacks on Tuesday after the work-group session (squat / deadlift / lunge variants, 4–6 reps × 3–4 sets). Maintenance follows Thursday's easy / medium-long run (single-leg, hip / core / calf, 20–30 min). Run first, lift after — the run is the priority.

Standing rule (shoe rotation): easy / recovery / Thu / daughter run → Altra Torin. Tuesday quality + Friday Z2 long → Asics Megablast. Block 2 specificity runs 40 km+ starting Jun 22 → Adidas Evo SL on audit (sized up for foot swell); primary fit-check Thu Jun 4 (explicit toe-box A/B after the May 29 pinky-toe signal). Race-shoe verdict after the 60–70 km specificity runs, target ~Jul 20.

After Berlin · the second arc

Five to six weeks between a 161k and a fast 30k.
The body decides what's possible.

Weeks 1–2 post-Berlin are full recovery: walks, easy spinning, jogging only when it feels right. Weeks 3–4 rebuild aerobic and reintroduce one short tempo. Race week is light — Lidingö is run on whatever fitness remains.

The point is not to PR. It's to enjoy a fast 30k off ultra fitness while the body is still tuned. If recovery from Berlin runs longer than expected, Lidingöloppet drops to a hard long run with a finish line — Berlin was the year, and Lidingö is the cooldown.
