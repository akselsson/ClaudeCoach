Italy deload done —
re-entering specificity.

The two-week Italy deload closed out clean. Running volume stayed deliberately low through the back half (three easy runs, all on the Megablast, all 9–13 km and flat). The Jul 3 run cooked into the threshold band via heat drift and Megablast free-pace (TRIMP 189, 38.7% threshold-zone time) — not deliberate training, just a warm run that drifted — and the shins took it fine, which matters. The Megablast broken-in read is now resolving positive: free pace on flat road, same free-pace signature the Evo SL showed. Aerobically fresh. Musculoskeletally pre-loaded by ~1,880 m of Italian vert in week 1.

Kundo on summer break Jul 6 → Berlin. The Tuesday work-group anchor is gone for the whole remaining build. Every remaining Tuesday is self-directed — a controlled session (light threshold or hill economy), never forced solo-hard the day before a big weekend. Quality that matters for a 161 km race is the weekend long/B2B volume; this is low-cost in specificity.

Gear rotation standing (RATIFIED Jun 30): Evo SL Silver → race day + long runs / big-specificity weekends only. Cushioned daily trainers (Lone Peak 9+ trail, Torin / Megablast road) → all easy/recovery volume. The first Evo SL long since the Jun 21 shin signal is Sat Jul 11 re-entry long (~30–34 km) — the shin re-watch. First true big B2B (50+35 km) confirmed for Jul 18–19, with an adaptive downgrade to ~42–45+30 km if the Jul 11 re-entry long reads heavy or shins speak.

Adopted May 10: 2× / week strength. Heavy stacks on Tuesday after quality (squat / deadlift / lunge, 4–6 reps × 3–4 sets). Maintenance follows Thursday's run (single-leg, hip / core / calf, 20–30 min). Run first, lift after.

After Berlin · the second arc

Five to six weeks between a 161k and a fast 30k.
The body decides what's possible.

Weeks 1–2 post-Berlin are full recovery: walks, easy spinning, jogging only when it feels right. Weeks 3–4 rebuild aerobic and reintroduce one short tempo. Race week is light — Lidingö is run on whatever fitness remains.

The point is not to PR. It's to enjoy a fast 30k off ultra fitness while the body is still tuned. If recovery from Berlin runs longer than expected, Lidingöloppet drops to a hard long run with a finish line — Berlin was the year, and Lidingö is the cooldown.
