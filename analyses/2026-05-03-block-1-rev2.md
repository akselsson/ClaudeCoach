## What I'm watching for

Re-evaluate before locking Block 2 (week of Jun 8) on these signals:

- **Resting HR trend through May.** >5 bpm above baseline persisting >3 days = back off. Especially relevant Wed + Thu of race week (post-fever DNS gate).
- **HR-to-pace drift at HR 140.** Should be flat or improving by end of May. Worsening = under-recovered.
- **Sleep quality.** Ultra recovery shows up here first.
- **Tuesday work-group response in week 5.** If 5×6 min threshold lands at HR 158–163 cleanly with no excessive next-day fatigue, the build is healthy. If HR runs above 165 at the prescribed pace or Wed/Thu legs feel wooden, drop the second quality day from Block 2's plan.
- **Niggles** — Achilles, anterior shin, calves especially. Apr 28 intervals were neuromuscularly higher-stress than recent weeks, and the Tuesday return in week 5 will be the next test.
- **Friday long run logistics.** Make sure the schedule actually holds — if work emergencies repeatedly bump the long run to Saturday, that's a signal to revisit the standing schedule, not to keep papering over it.
