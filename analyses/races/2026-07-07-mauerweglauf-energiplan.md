## Race facts that drive the design (all 2026-confirmed)

- **162,1 km, start 06:00 lör 15 aug, 30h cutoff, one overnight.** Start/finish Friedrich-Ludwig-Jahn-Sportpark.
- **2026 is counter-clockwise (moturs)** — confirmed against 100meilen.de course + station pages (Berlin.de wrongly says clockwise; organizer is authoritative). North first (Wilhelmsruh km7,7 → Frohnau km27,8), west (Sacrow km70), south (Teltow km102), inner-city landmarks **last** (East Side Gallery km148, Checkpoint Charlie km153). The km marks in the plan are the moturs marks — do **not** reverse.
- **26 aid stations (VP)**, spacing ~5–9 km, every one a cutoff control. **3 drop bags (Wechselstellen):** VP6 Ruderclub Oberhavel **km33**, VP12 Schloss Sacrow **km70 (~halvvägs)**, VP17 Sportplatz Teltow **km102**.
- **Flat/fast** but mixed surface — asphalt + gravel + broken slabs + **sand sections**; single named climb **Dörferblick ~km123** (walk it).
- **Clock at sub-20h → finish ~01:52 sön, so ~75% of the race is daylight.** Darkness only falls ~21:00 (~km128); night is just the final ~4,5h. **Heat (aug day ~24–25°C, hot years 30°C+) is the bigger threat than the night.** Night-gear (pannlampa + reflexväst) mandatory 21:00–06:00 → collect at VP17 (km102), on by VP21.

## Fuelling architecture

**Backbone = U Sport**, counted in whole **90 g portions** (Patrik 2026-07-07: use the ready pouch as the unit — easier to carry + pour than loose scoops). Container = Umara's 100 g pouch **or** a self-filled ziplock à ~90 g (Clas Ohlson 0,3 L, art. 44-5353-6). **Serving rule: 1 portion → emptied across BOTH 450 ml softflasks (~45 g/flaska, ~10% mix) = one refill per ~900 ml.** Empty a fresh portion when the mix runs out, else just top up water. Ziplocks give free portioning — pre-make half-dose (45 g) bags for the diluted late mix and pre-mixed heat bags (90 g U Sport + U Hydrate/salt). Gels (Umara/Maurten) + U Bar top up. **Carb curve, not a flat number** — lean on the drink + cola so gels stay low, especially late when the gut tires:

| Phase | km | Mål g CHO/h |
|---|---|---|
| Fresh, daylight | 0–70 | ~88 |
| Post-halfway | 70–102 | ~78 |
| Dusk→dark | 102–142 | ~68 |
| Final push | 142–162 | ~60 |

**Totals:** ~1527 g CHO (snitt ~77 g/h) · **~11,4 U Sport-portioner (90 g)** → carry ~13 for margin, split across the 3 drop bags + start vest · **11 geler · 4 barer** · ~10 L vätska (normal temp). Gut tolerance for ~88 g/h early is proven (Patrik: worked at PRT 2025).

**Kit:** 2×450 ml softflask i Salomon Adv Skin 10 (1 portion split across both = ~45 g/flaska); extra soft-flask for water on hot days.

**Sodium:** U Sport already carries ~750 mg Na/portion ≈ 350–400 mg/h baseline — decent without add-ons in cool weather.

**Caffeine (budget ~400–500 mg):** held caffeine-free km0–95 to protect sensitivity → first 100 mg ~km96 (afternoon dip) → 30–100 mg per depot through the night, **preferably caffeine tablets (0 carb) so late g/h stays low** → optional U Intend 250 mg as the deep-low final boost (km148–162). U Intend is a pre-workout (250 mg caffeine, 0 carb), used as a one-shot, not fuel.

## Hot-weather protocol (>25°C, triggered at the km45 heat-check)

1. **Vätska:** follow the "Dryck/h VARMT" column (~+35%); carry extra plain-water soft-flask; douse head/neck; drink every 10 min.
2. **Natrium:** target 600–800 mg/h — 1 U Hydrate (500 mg) in one flask ~every 1,5h **or** 2 Saltstick FastChews (100 mg) every 45 min; U Salty (250 mg)/h on top if cramping.
3. **Mix:** dilute U Sport late if the gut protests; move carbs to gel/cola, salt+water separately. Lower g/h briefly beats shutting the gut down.
4. **Dropbags:** stock extra U Hydrate + FastChews/U Salty in **all three** so the protocol can start wherever the heat hits.
