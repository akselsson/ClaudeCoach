On the **Altra Torin 6** — a cushioned trainer, exactly the rotation's easy-volume shoe (Evo SL correctly stayed home). **Decision:** accept, no cost flagged. It's more stimulus than a pure recovery jog but well short of a workout, it was aerobically controlled, and it's on the right shoe. It does **not** change the trip — one modestly-warm 11 km two days out is absorbed by Friday's off day and today's rest. Noted only so a future session doesn't misread it as a planned quality session or as fatigue stacked into the B2B.
- **Sun Jul 12 (today) is deliberate rest, not the drafted easy medium 12–15 km.** **Decision:** correct call, endorsed — saving the legs for a 36 km mountain day tomorrow beats topping the weekly number. The ~76 km already hit target without it.
- **Re-entry front-load (from the Jul 8 sync) confirmed as the right shape.** The Tuesday self-directed quality was traded for the Wednesday long, and the week's hard days all landed by Thursday. **Decision:** ratified — this is the correct specificity-phase priority (weekend-shaped volume over a solo threshold session), and it bought the clean taper into the trip.
